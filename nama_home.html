OUR COLLECTION

At Nama we create unique handcrafted ceramics which follow a design aesthetic powered by elegance and simplicity. Our contemporary and artistic tableware will add a new charm to your kitchen and dining space. Made in small batches, our ceramics are one-of-a-kind with a distinctive personality and character. Inspired by our shared love for nature and colors of the earth, our slow pottery gives an eclectic touch to your modern lifestyle
