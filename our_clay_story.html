Our Clay Story

At Nama we are crazy about clay! We love what we do and would like to share and enthuse you with the same joy that we feel while creating these pieces. We believe that less is more and that tableware should elevate everything you serve in it.

Â 

Our pottery is designed, thrown, glazed, fired, photographed, marketed, sold, and shipped solely by our founder who strives to live, work, learn and thrive from her daily interactions.

Eco-consciousness and sustainability have always been at the heart of all our practices. We try to reduce waste and reuse and recycle most of our materials. Currently trying to reuse even our fired clay to give strength to the new clay, we love that our core materials are all natural and organic.

Nama is happy to say that we have almost managed to make our packaging plastic free but we still might sometimes need to bubble wrap our pieces to ensure they reach you safely over long distances. We strive to improve everyday.
